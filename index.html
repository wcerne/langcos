Welcome to Langcos. Set for launch April 2026

Langcos creates high-quality study guides for undergraduate physics and mathematics courses, designed to help students strengthen their understanding between lectures and exams. Each guide is intended as a supplement to standard university coursework, with an emphasis on problem-solving, intuition, and exam-relevant practice. Langcos is set for launch in April 2026.

Study Guides

Langcos study guides are available for individual purchase. They are carefully crafted, course-specific resources that consolidate the core ideas of a subject and focus on worked examples, structured practice problems, and clear explanations of common pitfalls.

Free sample chapters are provided so you can evaluate the style, depth, and difficulty before purchasing. Because the guides are delivered as instant digital downloads, all sales are final.

Subscriptions

For students who want ongoing support throughout the term, Langcos offers subscription plans. Subscriptions provide discounted pricing, live instruction, and regular access to guided help.

A Langcos subscription provides:

  • Discounted pricing on selected study guides
  • Access to live, small-group classes focused on applying the material
  • Subscriber-only office hours for getting unstuck and asking targeted questions

Subscription Tiers

  • Langcos Lite
    A flexible option for students who want structured support alongside their coursework.
    • Bundle discount on 4 core physics study guides
    • Discounted access to live classes
    • Access to subscriber office hours
  • Langcos Pro
    Designed for students who want the highest level of ongoing academic support.
    • Bundle discount on 4 core physics study guides
    • Discounted pricing on all additional study guides
    • Free entry to all live classes
    • Access to subscriber office hours
    • Access to Pro office hours with deeper, more focused support
