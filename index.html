Welcome to Langcos. Set for launch April 2026

Langcos helps undergraduate physics and mathematics students strengthen their understanding between lectures and exams. Our materials are designed to supplement standard university courses by focusing on problem-solving, intuition, and how concepts are actually tested on exams. Langcos is set for launch in April 2026, and will include:

  • Study Guides: High-quality, course-specific study guides that distill the core ideas of each subject and emphasize worked examples, carefully designed practice problems, and intuitive explanations. These guides are intended as a supplement to your main coursework—not a replacement. New guides will be released continuously.
  • Live Classes (twice per month): Small-group sessions focused on applying the material from the study guides, walking through representative problems, and addressing common points of confusion through guided practice and Q&A.
  • Subscriptions: Discounted access to core physics and mathematics study guides, free entry to live classes, and access to regular office hours—where you can work through homework, ask targeted questions, and get unstuck with real-time guidance.
