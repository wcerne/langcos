About Langcos

A calm, structured approach to learning math and physics.

Each Langcos pamphlet promises to deliver:

  • Reusable problem-solving patterns: standard methods and “next-step” logic you can apply immediately on assignments and exams.
  • Worked examples that teach strategy: not just the algebra—how to recognize what to do and how to check your result.
  • Common pitfalls called out early: the repeatable mistakes that cost points, explained before they happen.
  • Rigor without the textbook bloat: enough mathematical honesty to trust, written to be readable and efficient.
  • A layout built for review: compact structure you can skim quickly, return to when stuck, and use as a reference.

By email, you’ll get

  • Pamphlet downloads (free releases)
  • New releases (and updated versions)
  • Class announcements + sample clips
