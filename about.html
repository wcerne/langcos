About Langcos

A calm, structured approach to learning math and physics.

Mission

Langcos exists to make hard material feel organized and learnable: compact guides that are rigorous, readable, and focused on the ideas that actually unlock problems.

Who I am

I’m a graduate student in theoretical physics (based in Japan), focused on early-universe cosmology. I also love language learning—especially Japanese.

“Langcos” is a blend of those two interests: language learning + cosmology. The brand idea I’m leaning into is the “language of the cosmos”—learning the math and physics that describe the universe.

Teaching philosophy

  • Structure beats speed: a good map is better than more content.
  • Examples are the proof: learn the pattern, then generalize.
  • Pitfalls matter: most errors come from a small set of common confusions.
